GritsHub connects eligible individuals with local businesses to rescue quality food that would otherwise go to waste, creating a more sustainable and food-secure community.

How It Works

A simple process for businesses and recipients

1

Sign Up & Verify

Create an account and verify eligibility (EBT/SNAP for recipients; business details for donors).

2

List or Browse

Businesses list available food items; recipients browse and reserve what they need.

3

Coordinate Pickup

Set pickup times and locations that work for both parties.

4

Track Impact

See how much food you've rescued or received, and the environmental impact created.
